--- title: CVE Program Automation layout: page --- The goal of the [CVE Services](https://cveproject.github.io/automation/Services.html) is to simplify and automate the reservation of [CVE IDs](https://www.cve.org/ResourcesSupport/Glossary?activeTerm=glossaryCVEID) and the submission and uploading of [CVE Records](https://www.cve.org/ResourcesSupport/Glossary?activeTerm=glossaryRecord) to the CVE List for [CVE Numbering Authorities (CNAs)](https://www.cve.org/ProgramOrganization/CNAs).   ### Comments or concerns? Please use the [CVE Request Web Form](https://cveform.mitre.org/) and select “Other” from the dropdown menu.
